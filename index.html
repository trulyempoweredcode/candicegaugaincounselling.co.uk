How I Work

Therapeutic Approaches

I draw on a range of therapeutic approaches, tailoring each session to your individual needs, pace, and preferences.

Psychodynamic Therapy

Focuses on unconscious processes — thoughts and feelings — as manifested in present behaviour. This approach helps you develop self-awareness to understand how past influences create recurring behavioural and relational patterns.

Person-Centred Therapy

Establishes a non-judgemental therapeutic relationship. Rather than interpreting your behaviour, I foster conditions where you can explore your needs, goals, and personal growth naturally at your own pace.

Walk & Talk Therapy

Combines counselling with outdoor walking in South East London. Benefits include relaxation, reduced stress and anxiety, increased energy and focus, and easier self-expression through casual activity. Sessions take place in Betts Park during daylight hours near my office.

New clients: one in-office session required first · Ages 18+

Spirituality & Crystals

As a Certified Metaphysician, I explore experiences related to finding deeper meaning, purpose and connection. A small crystal selection is available in the therapy room — you may select and hold crystals during sessions as we explore their symbolism and your emotional responses together.

Creative & Integrative Methods

I also integrate CBT and Humanistic approaches, alongside creative tools including writing, drawing, sand tray work, toys, play-dough, oracle cards, spirituality resources, and crystals — offering different ways of working and creativity in the therapy room to suit your individual needs.

Your Journey

What to Expect

Taking the first step can feel daunting. Here is how the process works, so you know exactly what to expect.

1

Free Consultation

We begin with a complimentary 15–20 minute telephone or Zoom call to discuss your needs and determine if we are a good therapeutic fit for each other.

2

First Session

An assessment form is provided to help establish a direction for our work together. We can set goals together if you wish, creating a foundation for your therapy.

3

Ongoing Therapy

I will reflect observations back to you as we explore behavioural patterns and challenge belief systems together. You may encounter uncomfortable feelings — this is a normal part of healing.
